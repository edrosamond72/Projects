About Me

Introduction

With 17 years of teaching experience, I’m dedicated to helping students achieve their language goals. I hold a Cert Ed. Literacy Specialist from the University of the West of England and a TEFL certification. Over the years, I've guided countless students in mastering English, from beginners to advanced learners, both in England, Brazil and online across South America and Türkiye.

Personal Philosophy

My teaching philosophy centres on creating a supportive and engaging environment where students feel confident to speak and explore the language. I believe that learning English should be an enjoyable journey, and I tailor my lessons to fit each student's unique needs and goals.
